What is MESG ?

MESG offers a technology-agnostic infrastructure for decentralized app development. It provides infinite possibilities for developers and entrepreneurs to combine existing services to Execute Solutions instead of being constrained by the complexities of each step in the process.

Why build with MESG ?

Developers and Entrepreneurs will experience different benefits by using MESG.

Tap into the infinite possibilities of combining existing services to execute solutions instead of being constrained by the complexities of each step in the process.

  • Event Driven

    MESG’s architecture connects to all existing services and even the internet of things, to allow users to set up a series of tasks that will execute automatically once external events trigger it.

  • Decentralized

    The availability of decentralized processing allows you to pay only for what you use, and build applications without bottlenecks, overhead or a centralized point of failure.

  • Easy do develop

    MESG removes the steep barrier to implementing new technologies and services by removing the need to understand the context of technologies to benefit from their functionalities. Re-use the best solutions already created.

  • Technology agnostic

    All on and off-chain technologies of the present or future are compatible with MESG. Once a service is connected, it doesn’t need to be reconnected.

  • Efficiency

    You have no boilerplate code to maintain. Simply focus on your business logic.

  • Adaptable

    Updating your application is now as simple as plugging in your new service.

MESG will pay immediate rewards in time, energy and money.

  • Easy to Learn

    Build your company out of the best solutions already created. MESG provides infinite possibilities for Builders to create, share or combine existing connections to services to build new and better solutions.

  • Earn Money

    You can participate in 3 ways to earn GEMS. Pa Running a Node Creating a workflow Selling a workflow If you create value in our network, you could receive residual income.

  • Reduce Waste

    MESG allows business builders to create using a pay-as-you go model. MESG reduces overhead.

  • Set and Forget

    Using the technologies connected to MESG, you can design a chain reaction of tasks that executes exactly when you want them to. Make workflows automatic.

  • Unlimited Compatibility

    MESG is compatible with any technology on or off the blockchain, and allows you to combine existing services to Execute Solutions instead of being constrained by the complexities of each step in the process.

  • Scalable - Grow exponentially

    A pay-as-you-go model allows you to pay only for what you use, so that you can avoid large subscriptions and huge overhead, while decentralized processing and the network effect allows provides the potential for exponential growth.
